Empowering the next generation of developers

At GDSC Unilag, we're dedicated to nurturing the next generation of developers. Through innovation and collaboration, we provide the tools and support needed for success. Join us as we shape the future of technology together.

Join our community

Who we are

GDSC Unilag, affiliated with Google Developer Student Clubs, is a vibrant community of developers and tech enthusiasts dedicated to innovation and growth. As part of the global GDSC network supported by Google, we offer a platform for students to explore, learn, and create with the latest technologies. Through hands-on workshops, collaborative projects, and industry mentorship, we empower our members to become leaders in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Join us on this exciting journey as we leverage the resources and expertise of Google to shape the future of technology together.

Read more about GDSC Unilag

Why should you join GDSC?

  • Experience

    Get hands-on experience with Google Developer Technologies by building real-world projects. Work on projects with a team of like-minded developers. Learn from Google experts through workshops, hackathons, and events.

  • Skill development

    Develop your skills in areas such as Android development, web development, cloud computing, and machine learning. Gain experience with the latest Google Developer tools and technologies. Learn from industry professionals and mentors.

  • Networking

    Connect with other developers from around the world. Build relationships with Google Developers Experts and mentors. Find internship and job opportunities at Google.

  • Career Opportunity

    Prepare yourself for a career in tech by gaining valuable skills and experience. Get noticed by potential employers by building a strong portfolio of projects. Connect with recruiters at GDSC events.
